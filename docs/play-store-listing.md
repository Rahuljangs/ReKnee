## Full Description

ReKnee is your intelligent ACL recovery companion — guiding you through every phase of rehabilitation after ACL reconstruction surgery.

Recovering from ACL surgery is a long, challenging journey that can take 9-12 months. ReKnee combines evidence-based rehabilitation protocols with AI-powered daily check-ins to keep you on track, motivated, and safe throughout your recovery.

KEY FEATURES

- Daily AI Check-Ins: Chat with your personal recovery companion about how your knee is feeling. ReKnee understands your current phase and provides contextual guidance and encouragement.

- 5-Phase Exercise Program: Evidence-based exercises tailored to your current recovery phase — from early protection through return to sport. Each exercise includes step-by-step instructions.

- Safety Monitoring: Built-in screening for Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) symptoms — a potentially life-threatening complication after knee surgery. Instant alerts if concerning symptoms are detected.

- Progress Tracking: Track your daily symptoms, pain levels, and exercise completion. Watch your progress over time.

- Phase-Specific Guidance: The app automatically determines your recovery phase based on weeks since surgery and functional milestones.

- Daily Motivation: Phase-specific motivational quotes and practical recovery tips to keep you going on tough days.

REHABILITATION PHASES
- Phase 1: Protection & Early Motion (Weeks 0-2)
- Phase 2: Early Strengthening (Weeks 2-6)
- Phase 3: Progressive Strengthening (Weeks 6-16)
- Phase 4: Sport-Specific Training (Weeks 16-24)
- Phase 5: Return to Sport (Weeks 24+)

IMPORTANT DISCLAIMER
ReKnee is a wellness companion and adherence tracker. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or replace professional medical advice. Always consult your physician or physiotherapist before making changes to your rehabilitation program. The AI companion cannot prescribe medication, diagnose conditions, or authorize phase changes.
