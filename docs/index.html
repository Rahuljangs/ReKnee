Privacy Policy

ReKnee - ACL Recovery Companion · Last updated: June 13, 2026

ReKnee ("we", "our", or "the app") is a wellness companion and adherence tracker designed to support individuals recovering from ACL reconstruction surgery. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Important: ReKnee is not a medical device. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or replace professional medical advice. Always consult your physician or physiotherapist for clinical decisions.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use ReKnee:

Account Information

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • Profile photo URL (from your Google account)

Health and Recovery Information (self-reported)

  • Surgery date and graft type
  • Daily symptom reports (pain levels, swelling, stiffness)
  • Exercise completion logs
  • Conversational messages with the AI companion

Device Information

  • Push notification tokens (for daily check-in reminders)
  • Device type and operating system version

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Personalized Recovery Guidance: Your surgery date and symptom data are used to provide phase-appropriate exercise recommendations and conversational support.
  • AI-Powered Conversations: Your messages are processed by Google's Gemini AI to generate contextually relevant responses. Messages are sent to Google's servers for processing and are not stored by Google for model training (per our API agreement).
  • Safety Monitoring: Your symptom reports are automatically screened for signs of Deep Vein Thrombosis (DVT) and other medical emergencies to display safety alerts.
  • Push Notifications: To send daily check-in reminders and important recovery updates.
  • Service Improvement: Aggregated, de-identified usage data may be used to improve the app experience.

3. Third-Party Services

ReKnee uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database storage (Cloud Firestore), and serverless functions (Cloud Functions). Data is stored in Google Cloud data centers with encryption at rest and in transit. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI: Processes conversational messages to generate AI responses. Symptom data is included in prompts for context. Gemini API Terms
  • RevenueCat: Manages in-app subscriptions. Receives your anonymous user ID and purchase status. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Expo Push Notifications: Delivers push notifications via Expo's notification infrastructure. Expo Privacy Policy

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS.
  • Data at rest is encrypted on Google Cloud servers.
  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party.
  • Access to user data is restricted to essential app functionality only.

5. Data Retention

  • Your account data is retained for as long as your account is active.
  • Daily logs and chat history are retained for the duration of your recovery period (up to 18 months) and may be deleted upon request.
  • If you delete your account, all associated data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: Stop using the app at any time. You can sign out and request data deletion.

7. Children's Privacy

ReKnee is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete it.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
